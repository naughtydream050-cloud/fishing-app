# Content Rules

Goal: publish original SPEC AI posts that feel useful, specific, and non-spammy.

Allowed:
- original posts based on SPEC AI value
- reusable pattern extraction from public examples
- before/after workflows
- build-in-public progress
- product lessons
- lightweight educational posts

Not allowed:
- copied viral posts
- rewritten posts that keep the same wording
- fake metrics
- fake customer claims
- engagement bait
- automated likes, follows, DMs, replies, or quote posts
- more than 2 link posts/week

10/10 gate:
- clear SPEC AI relevance
- original wording
- one concrete idea
- useful without clicking
- no hype-only claim
- no copied structure from yesterday
- no spam signal
- fits X format
- CTA is soft or absent unless link slot is available
- matches current product facts

Reject:
- 9/10 or lower
- duplicate idea
- weak hook with no payoff
- vague AI productivity post
- any policy breach
