## Recommended Positioning

Account concept:
Useful Japanese discovery account for websites, AI tools, productivity tools, and small practical web apps.

Content lens:
- 今日の便利サイト
- 今日のAIツール
- メモ整理に使えるサイト
- 小さく仕事がラクになるWebアプリ

Tone:
- practical
- save-worthy
- lightly opinionated
- not corporate
- not hype-driven

SPEC AI role:
SPEC AI should be framed as the first featured tool, not the whole account.

## Why Japanese-First Is Better

The profile promise is Japanese. A Japanese-first post lets visitors understand the account in three seconds, improves trust, and makes the save/follow behavior more natural.

## Launch Week Adjustment

Each post should open with a useful-site angle, then explain the practical use case.

Avoid:
- "we launched"
- official product PR
- abstract SaaS wording
- English-only posts

Prefer:
- "今日の便利サイト"
- "こういう時に使える"
- "保存用にどうぞ"
- real UI image support
