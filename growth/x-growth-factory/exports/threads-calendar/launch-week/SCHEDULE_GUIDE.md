## Scheduling Steps

1. Open the matching `day-XX.md`.
2. Copy only the post text.
3. Open Threads compose.
4. Paste the post text.
5. Attach the matching PNG image.
6. Schedule one post per day.
7. In `launch-week-checklist.md`, change `unscheduled` to `scheduled`.

1. Change the post status to `posted`.
2. Open `launch-week-metrics.csv`.
3. Fill the row for that day.
4. Change metrics status in the checklist from `pending` to `recorded`.
