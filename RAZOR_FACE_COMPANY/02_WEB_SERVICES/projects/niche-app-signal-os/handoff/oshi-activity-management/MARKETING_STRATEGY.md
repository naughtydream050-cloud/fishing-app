# Marketing Strategy - Oshi Activity Management

Date: 2026-06-06

## Decision

Proceed with a validation-first marketing flow for the local-first MVP.

Do not position this as a finished SaaS. Position it as a small prototype that tests whether people who do推し活 want a single place for live logs, goods, travel costs, and memories.

## Primary Audience

- People who attend multiple live events or fan meetings per year.
- People who buy goods repeatedly and lose track of what they own.
- People who use calendar, notes, screenshots, spreadsheets, and DMs separately.
- People who want to keep memories without turning the tool into a public SNS.

## Core Message

推し活の予定、参戦記録、グッズ、遠征費、思い出メモを、ログインなしで1つにまとめる小さなローカル管理ボード。

## Positioning

- Local-first: browser storage only.
- Low-friction: no login, no account, no setup.
- Personal: public sharing is not required.
- Prototype: feedback-driven, not a polished paid product yet.

## Do Not Claim

- Do not claim reminders, sync, backup, ticket integration, companion sharing, or mobile app support.
- Do not claim official integrations with ticketing, Threads, note, or payment services.
- Do not imply data is backed up in the cloud.

## Validation Funnel

1. Threads-style DRY_RUN post validates the pain framing.
2. note article validates whether the problem story gets saves/comments.
3. Static LP validates whether people understand the product from one screenshot.
4. MVP trial validates whether localStorage-only is acceptable for first feedback.
5. Only after repeated positive signals, consider waitlist, sync, or paid features.

## First CTA

「ログインなしの試作版を触って、足りない項目を教えてください。」

This asks for feedback, not purchase. It is safer and better aligned with the current MVP.

## Success Signals

- Comments that mention their current workaround.
- Saves/bookmarks on problem-framing posts.
- Requests for fields such as lottery status, payment deadline, companion split, or photo attachment.
- Repeat mentions of "Notionだと重い", "スプレッドシートが続かない", or "スクショが散らかる".

## Kill / Pause Signals

- Responses only ask for generic calendar reminders.
- Users need cloud sync before any value is felt.
- Users treat it as a private diary only and do not need goods/travel cost management.
- The audience wants official ticket or payment integrations immediately.

## Next Execution

- Publish nothing automatically.
- Generate 3 DRY_RUN Threads post variants.
- Build note article outline.
- Build LP copy and section flow.
- Review with GPT/owner before any public post.
