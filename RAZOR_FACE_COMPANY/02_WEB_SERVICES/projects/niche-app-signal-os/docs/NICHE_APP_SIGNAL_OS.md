# Niche App Signal OS

Niche App Signal OSは、Threadsで日々のニッチ需要を検証する内部運用システムです。投稿自動化そのものではなく、リサーチ、仮説、投稿案、画像カード、反応学習、Web化候補、note化候補を蓄積します。

## Departments

1. Research Department
2. Viral Pattern Department
3. Memory Department
4. Trend Timing Department
5. Planning Department
6. LLM Council Department
7. Copywriting Department
8. Design Department
9. Risk Control Department
10. Publishing Department
11. Analytics Department
12. Executive Department

各部署は `output/reports/*.json` で連携します。
